Controls:

Left: A/Left Arrow

Right: D/Right Arrow

Fast Drop: S/Down Arrow

Rotate CW: E/X

Rotate ACW: Q/Z


Make a chain of 4 blocks of 1 colour to clear them.

Any adjacent ground tiles will be blown up!


Matching or more blocks in a chain powers up a ball of energy to destroy even more tiles!


Make long chains and trigger chain reactions to break even more ground.


Making lots of incomplete groups of 3 can help when trying for large chain reactions
